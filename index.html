Get A Boost

Architecting a Life

A study in what sets me up to weather the cycles of life. What best positions me to be able to respond when called upon. What provides the flexibility to be accountable to others and live the wild life I want to live.

Body

Do something that might crush you.

Pay attention to what you put into your body. Good energy in, good energy out. Challenge yourself physically on a regular basis. It is clear that my body is happiest when I explore its limits. Continue to search for balance between time at the desk/screen and natural movement.

Mind

Study something hard.

Keep the mind sharp. Work/career seems like the most obvious way to practice this, but as of late I've found this to be a struggle and have had to seek challenge in other areas. Always be reading for both knowledge and pleasure. Non-fiction during the day and fiction before bed.

Spirit

Find the connection, not the difference.

By far the segment I understand the least and the one I will have to explore alone. The practice of being connected to this moment, the things in front of me and inside of me. Best I can tell this area will be fed by me answering my desire to explore; places, people and ways of being. If my spiritual practice is isolating me from other beings/things, something is amiss.

Areas To Study

  • Anatomy - blend care for my body with the natural curiosity about how it works.
  • Psychology - how/why does the mind get so trapped in negative cycles. How to help others break out?
  • Neuroscience - how has our body/mind evolved to be in its current state. How can I optimize to support this architecture.
